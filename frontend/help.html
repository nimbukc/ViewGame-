Help Guide

How to Search for a Team

On the Home page, type a Bundesliga team name in the search bar and click Search. For example try typing Bayern or Dortmund.

How to Read the Statistics

After searching, you will see a stats panel showing the team's points, wins, losses, draws, goals scored, and goals conceded for the current season.

How to Save a Favorite Team

After searching for a team, click the Save to Favorites button. The team will be saved and will appear in the Saved Favorites section at the bottom of the page.

How to View Recent Matches

The Recent Matches section on the Home page shows the latest Bundesliga match results. You can scroll through them using the slider.

Limitations

ViewGame currently supports Bundesliga data only. Data is provided by OpenLigaDB and may occasionally be delayed or incomplete.
